Who is resume.com for?


resume.com is a tool for anyone tired of fighting with formatting their application document in a word processor. It’s even more valuable to job seekers pressed for time or worried about how the wording of their resume reflects on them as candidates. resume.com can dramatically cut down on the time it would take to create a resume on your own. We’ve heard success stories from job seekers in all industries. Interns, juniors, mid-level staff, all the way to c-suite executives. Even students writing resumes for college applications.
